Dish

Attributes:
diameter (float)
totalNumber (integer)
dirtyNumber (integer)
stackable (boolean)



Methods:

inUse (multiplies dirtyNumber by 2)
shelved (uses stackable: if stackable is true then uses totalNumber integer minus dirtyNumber)
dishwasherArea (uses diameter multiplied by dirtyNumber + .75)
tableArea (multiplies diameter by 1.5)
